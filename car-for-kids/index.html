  1. Fast Pickup & Towing – At Time & Place Convenient for You

  3. Running or Non-Running Conditions Accepted

Car for Kids is one of the trusted legitimate car donation programs that seeks to uplift children all over the United States. Our mission is to transform the lives of our nation’s children and their families. Your vehicle donation becomes critical funding for countless services, such as medical treatment, access to free educational and social service resources, emotional well-being projects, and essential quality of life programs.
